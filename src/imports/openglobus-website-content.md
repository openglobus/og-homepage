3. Блок: Core Capabilities

Каждый из разделов представлен в виде прямоугольника, все они равного размера. Каждый такой прямоугольник состоит из иконки, заголовка и пояснения

**Multi-Resolution Terrain Rendering**
Seamless integration of high-resolution terrain datasets, including custom and procedural models. Supports dynamic terrain updates, elevation profiling, slope analysis and real-time landscape interaction.

**Geospatial Data Visualization**
Comprehensive support for satellite imagery, vector layers, spatial objects and points of interest. Compatible with multiple coordinate systems and planetary reference models, including Earth, Moon and Mars.

**Real-Time 3D Object Visualization**
Efficient rendering of large numbers of dynamic 3D objects — from simple primitives to complex glTF models. Includes terrain collision handling, scaling, lines, polygons, rays and object-level metadata interaction.

**Composite Mechanism & Robotics Visualization**
Visualization and manipulation of articulated systems such as robots, cranes and animated mechanisms. Provides structured control over rotations, transformations and kinematic behavior.

**High-Performance WebGL Architecture**
Optimized browser-based rendering pipeline designed for stability and scalability. Enables integration with external systems, simulation platforms and custom application frameworks.

**Cross-Platform WebGL Deployment**
Runs directly in modern browsers across desktop and mobile environments without native installation. Ensures consistent rendering behavior across operating systems and device types.

**Drone Systems**
Real-time visualization of aerial vehicles, telemetry streams and terrain interaction. Supports drone navigation, monitoring systems and spatial control interfaces.

**Industrial & Energy Infrastructure**
Visualization of large-scale industrial assets such as pipelines, mining operations and energy facilities, enabling terrain-aware spatial planning and complex geospatial system modeling.

**Scientific Research**
Planetary-scale terrain rendering and geospatial modeling for academic research, environmental analysis and experimental visualization workflows.

**Planetary Visualization**
High-precision rendering of Earth, Moon, Mars and custom planetary bodies with flexible projection handling and scalable terrain integration.

**Simulation & Game Engines**
Foundation for browser-based simulation environments and interactive 3D systems combining terrain rendering, dynamic objects and real-time control logic.
